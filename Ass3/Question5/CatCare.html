DOG CARE

Feeding

  1. Cats require taurine, an essential amino acid, for heart and eye health. The food you choose should be balanced for the life stage of your cat or kitten. Properly balanced foods will contain taurine.
  2. You will need to provide fresh, clean water at all times, and wash and refill your catâ€™s water bowls daily.
  3. Treats should be no more than 5-10% of the diet.
  4. Many people feed baby food to a cat or kitten who is refusing food or not feeling well Please read labels carefully: If the baby food contains onion or garlic powder, your pet could be poisoned.
  5. Take your pet to your veterinarian if signs of anorexia, diarrhea, vomiting or lethargy continue for more than two days.
